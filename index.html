This Website

Every year, a large number of students from various countries come to study at SUNY Oswego. Although school can be a major aid in the adjustment process, but still most people are unfamiliar with how the living system works at new place.  where they can obtain their groceries, or how to organize a trip. This website addresses some of these issues, including housing, groceries, and transport in and from Oswego.
